# Water Rising

Open up the flood gates and let the water pour in with Minecraft, but the Water Rises. Have time to prepare, gathering resources and crafting new, water rising tailored items to help you in battle before the time ends and the water begins rising. Have fun and aquatic battles with the small selection of new items to help you. Who will be the last one standing to score the victory?

More information available on [plexion.dev](https://l.plexion.dev/waterrising)
